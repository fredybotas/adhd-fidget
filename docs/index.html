Rope Physics

Verlet-integrated particle chain gives the rope a natural, satisfying feel as the ball swings and settles.

Throw & Bounce

Flick the ball fast enough to snap the rope. It goes full free-ball mode and bounces off every screen edge.

🎨

Fully Customizable

Tune rope length, gravity, damping, ball size, color, and break threshold until it feels just right.

AI Prompt Waiting

Waiting for Claude, ChatGPT, or Cursor to respond? Fidget instead of tab-switching. ⌘G to summon, ⌘G to dismiss.

Lightweight
